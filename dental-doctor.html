About Dr. Poonam Pashine

Meet Dr. Poonam Pashine, a dental surgeon and hospital administrator with a team of super-specialist dental surgeons. With precision and compassion, she leads a journey of dental excellence.

Dr. Pashine brings a unique combination of clinical expertise and administrative excellence to Madhurama Netralaye & Dental Clinic. Her approach integrates advanced dental techniques with holistic patient care, ensuring optimal oral health outcomes for every patient.

Professional Philosophy

"It's not just about treating teeth; it's about touching lives. Every smile we restore represents a life transformed, and that's what drives our team to deliver exceptional care with compassion and precision."

With her MBA in Hospital Administration, Dr. Pashine fosters a collaborative, patient-centric environment where specialists work together seamlessly to deliver comprehensive dental solutions.

Holistic Dental Care Approach

Restoring smiles, transforming lives

Dr. Poonam Pashine's approach to dentistry integrates clinical expertise with genuine care for each patient's well-being. Her philosophy centers on several key principles:

  • Prevention First: Emphasizing education and preventive care to maintain optimal oral health
  • Comprehensive Solutions: Addressing both immediate concerns and long-term dental wellness
  • Patient Empowerment: Educating patients to become active participants in their oral health
  • Comfort-Centered Care: Creating a welcoming environment that eases dental anxiety
  • Lifelong Relationships: Building trust through consistent, high-quality care over time

From restoring smiles to preventive care, Dr. Pashine's holistic approach ensures that each patient receives personalized treatment tailored to their unique needs and lifestyle.
