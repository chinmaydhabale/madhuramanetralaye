Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our clinic

What services do you provide for eye care?

We offer comprehensive eye care services including cataract surgery with phaco machine, LASIK vision correction, glaucoma treatment, retina exams, squint correction, contact lens services, dry eye treatment, and pediatric ophthalmology.

What dental services are available at your clinic?

Our dental department provides a wide range of services including preventive care, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, orthodontics (braces and Invisalign), root canal treatment, gum disease treatment, oral surgery, and pediatric dentistry.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 90281 07222, booking online through IndiaOPD, or by visiting our clinic during working hours. For emergencies, we're available 24/7.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and identification when you visit. Our staff will be happy to assist you with insurance claims and paperwork.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow strict COVID-19 safety protocols including mandatory masks, temperature checks, frequent sanitization, social distancing in waiting areas, and HEPA air filtration systems. All our staff are fully vaccinated.
