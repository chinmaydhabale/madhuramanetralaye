Frequently Asked Questions About Our Facilities

What advanced equipment do you have for eye surgery?

Our facility features modern LASIK laser systems, advanced phaco machines for cataract surgery, digital diagnostic equipment including auto-refractometers, and computerized visual field analyzers. All equipment is maintained to the highest medical standards for optimal patient outcomes.

Are your operation theaters equipped with modern safety standards?

Yes, our operation theaters are fully equipped with sterile environments, advanced ventilation systems, and modern surgical equipment. We maintain international safety standards for all surgical procedures including cataract surgery, LASIK, and other eye treatments.

What dental facilities and equipment are available?

Our dental department features modern treatment suites with digital X-ray systems, advanced dental chairs, and comprehensive diagnostic equipment for all types of dental procedures from routine cleanings to complex treatments.

Do you have comfortable waiting areas for patients and families?

We have multiple comfortable waiting areas including a ground floor reception, second floor waiting room, and specialized pre-operative areas for surgery patients and their families. All areas are air-conditioned with modern amenities.
