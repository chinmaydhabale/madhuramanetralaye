About Dr. Durgesh Pashine

Dr. Durgesh Pashine stands as a distinguished figure in the realm of ophthalmology, renowned for his expertise in eye care and surgery. With over 15 years of experience, he has transformed the vision and lives of thousands of patients through his skillful hands and compassionate approach.

Dr. Pashine's journey in ophthalmology began at one of India's premier medical institutions, where he developed a passion for restoring vision and treating complex eye conditions. His dedication to excellence and patient-centered care has made him one of the most trusted eye specialists in the region.

Medical Philosophy

"I believe that vision is our most precious sense. My mission is to preserve and restore sight through cutting-edge technology combined with personalized care. Every patient deserves to experience the world in all its visual splendor."

Dr. Pashine is known for his gentle demeanor and ability to explain complex medical conditions in simple terms. He takes time with each patient to ensure they fully understand their condition and treatment options.

Advanced treatments for various eye conditions

Distinguished Training

Learning from the pioneers in ophthalmology

Training Under Padmashree Dr. Tatyarao Lahane

Dr. Durgesh Pashine had the privilege of training under the esteemed ophthalmologist, Padmashree Dr. Tatyarao Lahane, whose pioneering contributions have left an indelible mark on the field of ophthalmology in India.

Under Dr. Lahane's mentorship, Dr. Pashine honed his surgical skills and developed a deep understanding of complex eye conditions. Dr. Lahane's emphasis on precision, innovation, and patient-centric care has profoundly influenced Dr. Pashine's medical philosophy and practice.

This training period was instrumental in shaping Dr. Pashine's approach to ophthalmology, combining technical excellence with compassionate patient care.
